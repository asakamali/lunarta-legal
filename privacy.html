Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Lunarta Mama app ("Lunarta", "we", "us") collects, how it's used, and the choices you have. Lunarta is a tool that gives informational safety guidance about foods, medications, and products during pregnancy and breastfeeding.

No account required

Lunarta does not require you to create an account or sign in. There is no username, password, or personal profile stored on our servers.

Information stored only on your device

The following information is stored locally on your device (using standard app storage) and is never uploaded to a Lunarta-controlled database:

  • Your selected mode (pregnancy or breastfeeding)
  • Items you've saved for later reference
  • Your daily search count (used only to apply the free-tier limit)
  • Your subscription status

Uninstalling the app or clearing its storage permanently deletes this data. We have no way to recover it, because we never had a copy of it.

What we send to make the app work

When you search for an item, scan a barcode, or check recalls, the text of your query (and, for barcode scans, the barcode number) is sent to our backend server so it can:

  • Ask Anthropic's Claude API to generate a safety summary
  • Look up product information from Open Food Facts (for barcode scans)
  • Search public recall databases maintained by the U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC), U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA), and U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA)

These requests do not include your name, email, or any other identifying information — only the search text or barcode itself. All communication between the app and our servers uses encrypted HTTPS connections. Once requests are sent to third-party services such as Anthropic or Open Food Facts, they are subject to those services' own privacy policies. Public recall data is retrieved from U.S. government sources.

Subscriptions and payment

Lunarta Premium is sold as an auto-renewing subscription through the Apple App Store or Google Play. Payment, billing, and subscription management are handled entirely by Apple or Google — Lunarta never sees or stores your payment card details. We only receive confirmation that a subscription is active, so the app can unlock unlimited searches.

No advertising or tracking

Lunarta does not show ads, does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs, and does not sell or share your information with data brokers.

Children's privacy

Lunarta is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes — for example, if we add optional accounts in the future — we'll update this page and adjust the "last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Use the Help & Feedback option in the Lunarta app (tap the menu icon on the Search screen).
