Medical Disclaimer

Last updated: July 2026

In an emergency, don't use this app. Call 911 (or your local emergency number), or the Poison Control hotline at 1-800-222-1222 (U.S.), immediately.

Not medical advice

Lunarta summarizes publicly available guidance from sources such as the FDA, USDA, CPSC, ACOG, and NIH, and uses AI to generate plain-language summaries. This content is for general informational purposes only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

No doctor-patient relationship

Using Lunarta does not create a doctor-patient relationship between you and Lunarta, its developer, or any contributor. Always seek the advice of your physician, midwife, pharmacist, or other qualified health provider with any questions you have about a medical condition, medication, or product — especially before starting, stopping, or changing anything related to your health or your pregnancy.

AI-generated content can be wrong

Summaries in Lunarta are generated using an AI language model based on general public health guidance. AI-generated summaries may be incomplete, inaccurate, or out of date. Recall information is retrieved from official government databases, which may also be delayed or updated over time. Always verify anything important directly with your healthcare provider or the original source (such as the FDA, CPSC, or USDA).

Individual circumstances vary

Safety recommendations can depend on your individual health history, medications, allergies, and stage of pregnancy or breastfeeding. General guidance shown in the app may not apply to your specific situation.
