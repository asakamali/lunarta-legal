A.K. — Founder & Software Engineer

Software Engineer

A software engineer who experienced firsthand how difficult it was to find trustworthy, judgment-free safety guidance during pregnancy and breastfeeding, A.K. created Lunarta Mama to bring reliable, evidence-based information together in one place.

Lunarta Mama is the first product from Lunarta, an independent company focused on building trustworthy, evidence-based safety tools for families.

Questions? Use the Help & Feedback option in the Lunarta app, or email us at support@lunarta.io.
